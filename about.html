Our mission and story

Our Story

CampusConnect was born from a simple observation: students were missing out on amazing campus events simply because they didn't know about them. As a team of undergraduate students, we decided to solve this problem.

What started as a class project has grown into a platform used by thousands of students across our campus. We built this to make campus life more connected and engaging for everyone.

The Team

We're a group of Computer Science students passionate about creating solutions that matter. This project combines our skills in web development, design, and backend systems.
